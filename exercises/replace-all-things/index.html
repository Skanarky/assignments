THE SENATE: "NO MORE TAXES"

The senate decided on early Friday meeting that taxes will be no more needed!

The US has reached a GDP that will be covering everybody's needs. Soon we can also expect that there'll be no more need for working. While a lot layers of the society are celebrating the "happy" news, a lot of people are still sceptical if the new system will work in the longterm and what will be the moral consequences. We've seen in our times a lot of examples of people doing not really "nice" things when having no occupation. Like the old saying goes: "A man without a job - that is a crazy man!"
